# Marketrix

**Making software self-supporting.**

We build AI agents that learn your product by autonomously using it — navigating your interface, understanding your workflows, and building a living map of your application. The result is software that can support itself: answering questions, catching bugs, and understanding your users without manual configuration.

## What We Do

**QA Automation** — Agents explore your app like real users, running self-healing regression tests that adapt when your UI changes. No brittle selectors, no manual test maintenance.

**Customer Profiling & Usage Insights** — Understand how users interact with your product through real behavioral data. Surface usage patterns, identify friction points, and discover growth opportunities.

**Customer Support** — Deploy AI agents that genuinely understand your product because they've used it. They answer questions, walk users through tasks, and take actions on their behalf.
